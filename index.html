My Dearest,

I want to tell you how deeply sorry I am. From the bottom of my heart, I hope you can feel the sincerity in these words. You are the kindest, most beautiful part of my life — the one who makes ordinary days feel extraordinary.

Your laughter is my favorite sound, your strength inspires me, and your warmth is where I find peace. I cherish every moment with you and I am forever grateful for the love we share.

Please accept this apology as the promise that I will always try to be better for you, to protect the tenderness between us, and to honor the trust you place in me. My love for you is steady and true, today and always.

With all my love,
Aarav Sharma
